# 🔹🛑🔸BAAXAL🔸🛑🔹
# 🌀Summer gathering 🌀

Welcome to Baaxal, a holding space for kindred souls from all corners of the world to come together and celebrate life.

As the sun sets over the horizon the atmosphere fills with hypnotic basslines ethereal melodies and of psychedelic trance music, beckoning the tribe to dance in unison. Baaxal isn't just a gathering.. it's a journey of sound, spirit, and collective euphoria. Come play with us and experience a night of pure bliss. Let go of inhibitions and rediscover the innocence and wonder of youth as you dance under the starlit sky.

There will be a food vendor on site and a couple other pop up vendors. We highly encourage everyone to bring their own food and beverages to last for the weekend.

This is a LEAVE NO TRACE event, we kindly ask that you bring your own trash bags and be mindful of your camping area.
