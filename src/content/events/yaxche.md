# YAXCHE

In Mayan cosmology, the Yaxche represents the sacred Tree of Life that connects the physical and spiritual realms, with its branches reaching out towards the heavens and roots digging deep into the underworld.

The Yaxche symbolizes the Axis Mundi, a bridge between worlds, and embodies the cycles of life, death, and rebirth. It's a symbol of unity, harmony, and balance that reminds us of our connection to the natural world and the cosmos. In Mayan tradition, the Yaxche is revered as a source of wisdom, guidance, and spiritual growth.

Let's come together as one tribe under the ancient wisdom of the Yaxche, the Tree of Life, to celebrate the cosmic connection that unites us all. We long to explore the roots of our collective consciousness, reach for the leaves of higher states of being and spiritual growth, and connect with our community.
