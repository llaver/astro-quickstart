# 🔥 Gates to Xibalba II: The Return 🔥

A 3-day open-air Psytrance gathering celebrating the sacred bond between the living and the dead.

Hidden deep beneath the Earth lies Xibalbá, the Mayan underworld—a realm of shadow, mystery, and transformation. Once again, the Ajawab, Lords of Xibalba, stir in their domains as the veil between worlds grows thin.
This Day of the Dead, the Dimensional Mayan Tribe reopens the sacred gates for the second time. The path is calling once more. Those brave enough to walk it must descend through sound, fire, and darkness—crossing rivers of energy, light, and rhythm.

🌀 Gates to Xibalba is not just a festival—
It is a ceremony, a remembrance, and a return.
We gather to awaken ancestral memory and walk between dimensions, where ancient spirits meet modern beats.
This is a ritual of unity—of tribe, trance, and transformation.
🔥 At the Fire Ceremony, we’ll channel our highest vibrations through dance, intention, and collective energy.

TOGETHER, WE RETURN TO XIBALBA!
