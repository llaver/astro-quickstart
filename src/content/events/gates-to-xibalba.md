# 🔥 Gates to Xibalba 🔥

Hidden beneath the depths of the Earth, lies a world of darkness ruled by mysterious gods and terrifying creatures. This place is Xibalbá, the Mayan underworld ruled by the Ajawab, Lords of Xibalbá. The underground kingdom that houses the spirits of all human beings after their life on earth.

Mayans believed that this hidden place could be accessed through caves and cenótes. To get there, it involved making a long descent down a huge staircase, in addition to crossing rivers of water and blood. For this reason, the ancient Mayas buried their dead with water, food, weapons, and other objects as the souls would need provisions for their journey. Nowadays, as part of the Day of the Dead "Dia de Los Muertos" celebration, we provide offerings to the deceased so that they do not lack anything in the next life.

In this time of year, our ancestors prepare once again for their journey back home as the gateway to the subterranean world awaits to be opened.

An altar will be set to honor our deceased loved ones. Everybody is welcome to bring photographs, candles, seasonal flowers, a sample of their favorite drinks and meals, a special object, drawings, etc., and place them in the altar as we welcome the pixano'ob (souls) who come visit from the underworld.

Join us as we celebrate this sacred Psytrance gathering between the living and the dead. Let's light the sacred fire at the opening ceremony and offer our stomps and dances as we channel our highest vibrational states as one tribe.

There will be a food vendor on site and a couple other pop up vendors. We highly encourage everyone to bring their own food and beverages to last for the weekend.

This is a LEAVE NO TRACE event, we kindly ask that you bring your own trash bags and be mindful of your camping area.
